Future Minds: NextGen Design Lab

A flagship Solutions WaterMinds program that equips young learners with creativity, design thinking, and 3D modeling skills to become tomorrow’s innovators.

Introduces innovation & design thinking (Grades 9–13 and school leavers).
Hands‑on with SolidWorks 2019 and solid modeling techniques.
Students design a product idea, present, and learn IP basics.
Enroll Your School View Modules

1) Creativity & Innovation Basics · 2h

What is creativity? Pathways to invention, case studies, and the inventor log book.

2) Design Thinking for Students · 2h

Problem identification, brainstorming, rapid prototyping.

3) Intro to SolidWorks 2019 · 3h

Installation, interface overview, basic shapes.

4) Levels of Technology Readiness · 4h

For Creativity, Inventions and Innovations.

5) Designing an Invention · 4h

Each student proposes and models a product idea.

6) Final Presentation & IP · 2h

Project showcase, peer feedback, intellectual property concepts.

Total program duration: 17 hours (2 days). Initial pilot: 10 programs in Gampaha & nearby regions.
